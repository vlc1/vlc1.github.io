{y∗=yn+τ2f(tn,yn),yn+1=yn+τf(tn+1/2,y∗)

σrk2:z↦1+z+z22.

μ=|σrk2|2

μ:(x,y)↦(1+x+x2−y22)2+[y(1+x)]2.
